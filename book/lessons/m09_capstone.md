# Module 9 — Capstone: Make It Yours

**Runs on Google Colab.** You'll need a free Google account.

This is where it becomes *your* model. Instead of Shakespeare, you bring your own
text corpus — song lyrics, code, a book you love, your own writing — and train
the same GPT on it. The model will pick up the vocabulary, rhythm, and quirks of
whatever you feed it.

You've done every step before; this module is about ownership. Pick a corpus,
train, sample, and see your model speak in a voice you chose. The notebook keeps
the model small so everything stays fast and the optional checkpoint download
stays reliable.

As with Modules 7 and 8, the final cell prints a **submission token** to paste
into the grader. The capstone also offers an **optional checkpoint download** —
a small saved model file you can keep as proof of the model you trained, or
reload later.
