Why now

The age of the open Internet is ending. The age of guarded AI has begun.

In 2025, the United States published the AI Diffusion Framework. It sorts every country in the world into tiers, and decides who gets frontier models and who does not. Recent restrictions have already cut access for whole regions to capable models from American labs, including those that power the products you and your friends use every day.

India is not on the top tier. We were warned. And we are not the only country reading the room. From this point forward, foundation AI is a sovereign capability, not a global commodity.

The Internet

Open by design.

TCP/IP, HTML, HTTP, SMTP. The protocols that built the web were published as drafts and adopted by everyone. Anyone could spin up a server. Anyone could read what anyone else had written. No country could meaningfully gate it.

That openness is why India produced Flipkart, Paytm, Razorpay, Zerodha, and a million indie developers. We did not need permission. We built on a public substrate.

Foundation AI

Guarded like a strategic asset.

A frontier foundation model costs hundreds of millions of dollars in compute to train and is governed like nuclear technology. The weights are not free. The training data is not public. Access is licensed, rate-limited, and revocable.

AI is not the next Internet. It is the next national capability. The Internet was infrastructure for connecting people. Foundation AI is infrastructure for thinking. No country that depends on a foreign provider for that is independent.

A fork in the road

Two paths for a young Indian engineer in 2026.

Path A. Get certified.
  • Memorize a vendor's API and earn a badge.
  • Pay for tokens in foreign currency.
  • Wait for quota. Hope the policy winds stay favourable.
  • Build a wrapper. Watch a model update break it overnight.
A career renting capability that someone else owns.
Path B. Learn to build them.
  • Implement a neuron. Derive a gradient. Watch loss fall.
  • Write attention with your own hands. Train a tiny GPT.
  • Understand every line. Modify any layer. Read every paper.
  • Be the person who ships the next India-trained model.
A career building capability that India owns.

Certifications make you employable in a vendor ecosystem. Construction makes you sovereign in your own.
